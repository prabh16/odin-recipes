Channa Masala

Description

Channa Masala is a hearty and flavorful North Indian dish made with chickpeas simmered in a spiced tomato-onion gravy. It's a popular vegetarian dish that's both filling and nutritious, commonly served with rice or flatbreads like roti, naan, or bhatura.

Packed with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, garam masala, and turmeric, this dish delivers a bold and comforting flavor profile. It's perfect for cozy dinners or as part of a festive Indian meal, and it's easy to make with pantry staples.

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 1 tablespoon rice flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
